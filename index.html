Introducing EFG Games!

Clifton Kpueh, the founder of EFG Games, established this PC-based website in 2024 with the aim of offering a diverse selection of educational games for children of all ages. His inspiration for creating EFG Games stemmed from his passion for providing accessible and high-quality education to everyone. The games available on the platform cover various subjects such as math, reading, science, and more, allowing kids to have fun while developing valuable skills.

In addition to EFG Games, Clifton is also the founder of Tutorants, a community and mentorship-based platform that provides 24-hour tutoring to students in need of academic support. Tutorants has 5,000 expert tutors specializing in all academic disciplines. Clifton's journey towards creating EFG Games began in Monrovia, Liberia, where he was born, and his dedication to education was further fueled by the loss of his little sister and the hardships his family faced. With a degree in Organizational Communications from Metropolitan State University.

Clifton is determined to utilize EFG Games as a means to provide opportunities, resources, and motivation to children worldwide. His ultimate goal, alongside Tutorants and EFG Games, is to foster a love for learning in every child. Join us at EFG Games and embark on an exciting educational journey¬†together!
